Loaded Veggie Pizza

Prep: 30 mins Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup bell peppers (red, yellow, green – sliced)
  • 1/4 cup sweet corn
  • 1/4 cup olives
  • 1/4 cup onions (sliced)
  • 1 tsp chili flakes & oregano
  • 1 tsp olive oil

Procedure

Preheat the oven to 200°C (392°F). Spread pizza sauce over the base. Top with cheese and all the chopped veggies. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle oregano and chili flakes. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and the base is crisp.

Tips

Use preheated oven for better crust. Don’t overload with veggies to avoid sogginess. Add paneer or jalapeños for a twist.
