Paneer Tikka Masala

Prep: 30 mins Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 cup thick yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 onion (pureed)
  • 1 tomato (pureed)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

Marinate the paneer cubes in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt for 30 minutes. Grill or pan-fry the marinated paneer until golden brown. In a separate pan, heat oil and sauté the onion and tomato puree until the oil separates. Add the grilled paneer to the masala, mix well, and simmer for 5 minutes. Garnish with cream or coriander leaves and serve hot.

Tips

Use thick hung curd for a rich marinade. Grill paneer on high heat for a smoky flavor. Add a splash of cream for extra richness.
